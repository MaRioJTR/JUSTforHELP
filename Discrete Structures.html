    Propositions

    Propositions is a declarative sentence that is either"TRUE" or "FALSE".

    ببساطة لازم تكون الاجابة ب صح او غلط مينعش اي اجابة تاني

    مشينفعش الاجابة تكون قيمة او رقم او مكان او زمان الخ

    Examples of Propositions

    (A) Moon is made of green cheese

    Answer is FALSE but it still Proposition

    (B) Cairo is a capital of Egypt

    Answer is clearly TRUE it's a Proposition

    (C) 1 + 1 = 2

    TRUE it's a Proposition

    (D) 0 + 0 = 5

    FALSE it's a Proposition

    Examples of not Propositions

    (A) Sit down!

    it's imperative form you gonna refuse or accept. it's NOT Proposition

    (B) What time is it ?

    Answer will be (the time now) not T or F. it's NOT Proposition

    (C) X + 1 = 2

    Answer is clearly "1" but it's not T or F. it's NOT Proposition

    (D) X + Y = Z

    Who knows XD. it's NOT Proposition

    Compound Propositions

    Compound Propositions are constructed from logical connectives and other propositions.

    These tools are used for propositions only.

    Negation tool is used for ONE proposition.

    Conjunction (and), disjunction (or), implies (if-then), biconditional (if and only if) are used to connect TWO propositions.All tools are Binary except Negation.
